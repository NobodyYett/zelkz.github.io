Privacy Policy

Bloom – Pregnancy Planner & Guide

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Bloom ("we," "our," or "us"). Bloom is a pregnancy companion mobile application designed to provide educational information, emotional support, and organizational tools throughout your pregnancy journey. Bloom is operated by Zelkz LLC ("Company").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Bloom mobile application (the "App"). By using Bloom, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use Bloom, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (or OAuth credentials if you sign in with Apple or Google)
  • Pregnancy Information: Due date, current pregnancy week, baby name (optional), baby sex (optional)
  • Health & Wellness Logs: Mood entries, symptoms you choose to track, and personal notes
  • AI Conversations: Questions you ask Ivy (our AI companion) and the context you provide during conversations
  • Calendar Information: Appointment details you add for calendar export functionality

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, frequency of use, daily AI question counts
  • Log Data: Error logs and performance data to improve the App

Information from Third Parties

  • Apple Sign-In / OAuth Providers: If you choose to sign in using Apple or another OAuth provider, we receive basic profile information as permitted by your privacy settings
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status and purchase history (we do not receive or store your payment card details)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Deliver personalized pregnancy guidance, track your progress, and respond to your questions through Ivy
  • Power AI Features: Send your questions and relevant context (pregnancy week, recent logs) to our AI provider to generate helpful responses
  • Process Subscriptions: Manage your premium subscription status and features
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Communicate with You: Send service-related notices and respond to support requests
  • Enforce Rate Limits: Track daily AI question usage to manage free and premium tier limits

AI & Third-Party Processors

Bloom uses trusted third-party services to operate. When you use the App, your data may be processed by:

OpenAI

  • Purpose: Powers Ivy's AI responses within Bloom
  • Data Shared: Your questions and contextual information (pregnancy week, mood, symptoms you've logged)
  • Privacy: Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Note: We do not use your data to train AI models

Supabase

  • Purpose: Secure authentication, database storage, and backend services
  • Data Shared: Account information, pregnancy logs, AI usage data
  • Privacy: Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Data Shared: User ID, subscription status, purchase events
  • Privacy: Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy
  • Note: Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple; we never receive your credit card information

Apple (App Store / In-App Purchases)

  • Purpose: Payment processing and subscription management
  • Data Shared: As required by Apple for purchase transactions
  • Privacy: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Data Retention & Deletion

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Pregnancy Logs: Retained until you delete your account or manually delete entries
  • AI Conversation History: We do not permanently store full conversation transcripts; questions are processed in real-time
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics purposes

Your Right to Delete

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time:

  1. In-App: Go to Settings → Delete Account
  2. Email: Contact support@zelkz.com

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will:

  • Delete your account and personal data within 30 days
  • Remove your data from our active databases
  • Note: Some data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently purged
  • Note: We cannot delete data already processed by third parties (e.g., anonymized analytics)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Server-side validation and rate limiting
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Medical Disclaimer

Bloom and Ivy are NOT medical devices, healthcare providers, or substitutes for professional medical advice.

  • Bloom provides general educational information and emotional support only
  • Ivy (our AI companion) does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatments, or provide medical advice
  • Always consult your doctor, midwife, or qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions
  • In case of emergency, contact emergency services (911) or your healthcare provider immediately
  • Never delay seeking medical advice because of something you read or received from Bloom or Ivy

The AI responses are generated by artificial intelligence and may contain errors or inaccuracies. You should independently verify any information before relying on it.

Children's Privacy

Bloom is intended for use by adults (18 years of age and older) and is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@zelkz.com. If we discover we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.
